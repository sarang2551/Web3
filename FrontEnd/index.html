About

MintMart is a online freelancing website dedicated for creators to get the credit and acknowledgement they need as they grow their portfolio.

  • Agent Side

    Why should I Mint?

    Creators cannot guarantee their work will be rightfully credited to their own. With minting, everyone in the blockchain acknowledges that you created this output.

  • Portfolio?

    How would I build one?

    Freelancers donâ€™t have a way to showcase their work with 100% credibility. With our website, minted outputs become more credible as it requires a transaction between an agent and a client.

  • Client Side

    Do I allow Minting?

    As a client, it would be nice to give credit to the agent as their hard work can't just be fully paid by currency. With this, you are doing things for the greater good.

  • Cost?

    Future Propositions

    We are planning to expand by having our own blockchain to minimize the cost of minting.
